Release Date & Platforms

Rockstar Games has officially confirmed that Grand Theft Auto VI will launch on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. The game represents the biggest leap in the series' history, with a living, breathing open world that pushes the boundaries of what's possible in interactive entertainment.

After years of speculation, the first official trailer dropped in December 2023, instantly becoming one of the most-watched gaming trailers of all time. The second trailer followed in early 2025, revealing even more about the game's stunning recreation of Vice City.

"GTA VI represents our most ambitious project ever. We're creating a world that feels truly alive, where every corner has a story to tell." — Rockstar Games

Setting & Map Details

Set in the fictional state of Leonida (inspired by Florida), GTA 6 features the return of Vice City — now a sprawling, modern metropolis surrounded by swamps, beaches, and rural areas. The map is rumored to be the largest in GTA history.

  • Vice City returns as a fully reimagined modern metropolis
  • Surrounding areas include swamps, beaches, keys, and rural towns
  • Dynamic weather system with hurricanes and tropical storms
  • Underwater exploration with detailed ocean floor
  • Interior spaces are more detailed than ever before

Characters & Story

GTA 6 introduces Lucia, the first female protagonist in the mainline series. Along with her partner Jason, players will experience a Bonnie and Clyde-inspired story of crime, ambition, and survival in modern-day Vice City.

The game features a dual-protagonist system, allowing players to switch between Lucia and Jason during missions, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to the narrative.

Key Characters

  • Lucia — A street-smart woman navigating the criminal underworld
  • Jason — Her partner in crime with his own complex backstory
  • A cast of supporting characters spanning every level of Vice City's social hierarchy
